# Product — ديمى لمواتير المياه

## Register

brand

## Brand Essence

**Engineered strength you can see.**

This is a brand for people who need water pumps to work, and work hard — not a brand trying to be cute about it. Every visual and verbal choice communicates: this equipment is built solid, the people selling it know exactly what they're talking about, and buying from them is a low-risk, high-confidence decision.

The site should feel like walking into a well-run industrial supplier's showroom — clean, well-lit, organized, nothing hidden — not a warehouse, and not a boutique.

## Brand Personality

**Bold Industrial.**

- Confident, not aggressive
- Precise, not decorative
- Strong structure — clear grids, clear hierarchy, nothing floats without a reason
- Machinery language is a feature, not something to soften (specs, materials, performance numbers displayed proudly)

Think: the visual confidence of an engineering spec sheet, not a lifestyle catalog.

## Voice & Tone — Direct & Technical

Specs and facts first. Persuasion happens through clarity and credibility, not through hype.

**Do:**
- Lead with numbers and specs (flow rate, power, materials, warranty)
- Short, declarative sentences
- Active voice: "يشغّل الماتور بكفاءة حتى في الجهد المنخفض" not vague claims
- Let the product's capability be the argument

**Don't:**
- Emotional/lifestyle copy ("عيلتك تستاهل الأفضل" style filler)
- Excessive exclamation marks or salesy pressure language
- Vague superlatives without a number backing them up ("الأقوى" without stating why)

**Sample line test:**
> ماتور Demy Water Jet Motors بقوة ٠.٥ حصان، تصميم مانع للصدأ، وضمان سنتين — مصمم للاستخدام اليومي المكثف.

That's the tone: fact, fact, fact, one confidence statement.

## Target Users

- **Homeowners** in Egypt (Cairo + nationwide) replacing or installing a water pump for their building or villa. Need reliability, want a trusted local supplier with a physical shop.
- **Plumbers & contractors** who specify and install pumps for clients. Need accurate specs, need to trust the supplier's expertise.
- **Farmers & agricultural users** needing submersible pumps for wells and irrigation. Price-sensitive but value durability above all.

## Design Principles

1. **Industrial clarity, not decoration.** Every element earns its place. Grids are explicit, spacing is generous but regular. Nothing ornamental that doesn't communicate product value.

2. **Specs as hero.** Product specifications are the primary content, not images or lifestyle photography. Display them prominently, consistently, and comparably.

3. **Trust through transparency.** Physical address, phone number, WhatsApp, 30 years of experience — make the real-world business front and center. The site exists to get the customer in the door (physically or on WhatsApp).

4. **Arabic-first, not Arabic-translated.** Copy is written in Egyptian Arabic naturally, not translated from English. Numerals in Arabic script (٠١٢٣) where appropriate.

5. **Confidence without hype.** No countdown timers, no "limited offer" pressure, no popups. The product and the expertise sell themselves.

## Anti-references

- **No lifestyle e-commerce** — this is not a fashion boutique or home decor site. No lifestyle photography, no aspirational mood imagery.
- **No SaaS/startup patterns** — no cookie banners, no chat widgets, no "book a demo" CTAs. The CTA is WhatsApp or walking into the shop.
- **No dark/gaming aesthetic** — this is a light, clean industrial showroom feel, not a dark mode tool.
- **No emotional copy** — no "عيلتك تستاهل الأفضل" style filler. Facts only.
- **No AI-generated generic** — no gradient text, no glassmorphism, no numbered section eyebrows (01/02/03), no hero-metric-template.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (contrast 4.5:1 body text)
- Reduced motion support for all animations
- RTL layout throughout
- Arabic numerals supported alongside English in search
- Works on low-end mobile devices (Egypt's mobile-primary internet usage)
- Lazy-loaded images for bandwidth conservation
